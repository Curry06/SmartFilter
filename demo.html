How to Install SmartFilter

  1. Download all extension files to a folder on your computer
  2. Open Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions/
  3. Enable "Developer mode" by clicking the toggle in the top right corner
  4. Click "Load unpacked" and select the folder containing the extension files
  5. Open Gmail and click the SmartFilter extension icon to start adding keywords
Note: This extension requires Chrome's Developer mode to install. It's designed to work exclusively with Gmail and respects your privacy by storing all data locally on your computer.
